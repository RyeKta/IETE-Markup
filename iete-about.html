Reaching the unreached

IETE is a leading professional society devoted to the advancement of Science & Technology in Electronics, Telecommunications, Information Technology, Computer Science and other related disciplines.

About Us

One of the important charter of IETE is to impart continuing engineering education to working professionals, in the field of electronics, telecommunication, computer & IT, at an affordable cost. Aim being to earn while you learn and targeting students who cannot afford engineering education at a regular engineering college.

  • Promote and conduct basic engineering and continuing technical education programmes for human resource development.
  • Helping students to learn and grow in the emerging field of Electronics and Telecommunication.
  • Provide a forum for discussion on national policies and to provide suitable inputs to policy makers
  • Organise conferences, symposia and workshops involving all concerned professionals, students and industry associations for the advancement of the Discipline.
  • Stimulate research and development in the Discipline
  • Bring out quality publications for all levels of readership.
